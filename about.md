# Better Pause

This mod has info about the level and creator in the pause menu. 
This mod is intended for star grinders so they can check information about 
levels mid attempt, but also has many other uses. 
The following statistics can be disabled in the config.

Contains:
- Difficulty and star count
- Level duration (exact time taken for verification)
- Level stats like likes/dislikes and downloads
- Creator of the level and their amount of creator points
- Level ID and update level came out in (roughly)
- Orb and diamond rewards

Enjoy!
